## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are both new and returning clients booking appointments with independent service businesses. They need a low-friction mobile-first flow to authenticate, choose a service, find a time, and confirm payment quickly.

### Brand Personality
Premium, calm, and trustworthy. The interface should feel refined and controlled, not loud or playful. Motion should be subtle and confidence-building.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, elegant booking experience with warm accent highlights. Visual language should emphasize clarity and reassurance while preserving a boutique, high-touch feel. AI recommendations should appear inline in the review step as smart, context-aware suggestions.

### Design Principles
1. Keep every decision booking-first: reduce friction and keep momentum toward confirmation.
2. Use subtle, purposeful motion for feedback and hierarchy, never decoration-heavy transitions.
3. Surface AI recommendations as assistive guidance, not interruptions.
4. Prioritize legibility and contrast with concise copy and strong scanning structure.
5. Preserve trust signals (clear totals, timing, and confirmation details) at every step.
